Most portfolio rewrites fail because content is scattered across components. A single source of truth makes updates much easier.

## Group content by intent

A clean model usually includes:

- Identity and hero copy
- Services and project cards
- Approach and pricing
- About, FAQ, and contact details

When these live in one typed file, you can swap dummy text for real copy without refactoring layout.

## Keep sections independent

Each section should render from data, not hard-coded markup. That makes it easy to remove pricing later, add testimonials, or promote a featured blog post.

## Write for scanning

Visitors rarely read every paragraph. Use:

- Short headings
- One idea per block
- Visible calls to action

This structure also maps nicely to SEO, because each section can align with meaningful keywords without keyword stuffing.
